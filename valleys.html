Valley of Flowers Trek

Valley of Flowers Trek - Heaven on Earth The Valley of Flowers boasts of its vibrant and charming flower beds. A colorful combinations of seasonal flowers that looks exactly like a color palette can be seen in this exotic flower valley in India. It is also a habitat to rare wildlife like snow leopard, blue ship, and Himalayan brown bear. The photogenic views of the colourful flower beds and the nearby Hemkund Saheb temple attract tourists. Location: Chamoli, Uttarakhand How to reach: The nearest airport is at Dehradun and railway connect at Haridwar. One needs to drive up to Joshimath from Dehradun and thereafter take a trek to Valley of Flowers.
