Vagator Beach is the northernmost beach of Bardez Taluka, Goa. It is across the Chapora River from Morjim in Pernem. To the south of Vagator is Anjuna, one of the first hippie trails of Goa Vagator beach is famous for its notorious rave parties that go well into the late hours of the night, its flea markets and the picturesque red laterite cliffs that overlook the beach.
